Indonesia Phone Number Format

Indonesian mobile numbers typically start with +62 (country code) followed by carrier prefix and subscriber number.

Major Mobile Operators in Indonesia

  • Telkomsel: Numbers starting with 811, 812, 813, etc.
  • Indosat Ooredoo: Numbers starting with 814, 815, 816, etc.
  • XL Axiata: Numbers starting with 817, 818, 819, etc.
