Nigeria Phone Number Format

Nigerian mobile numbers follow the format: +234 followed by a 10-digit number. Mobile numbers typically start with 070, 080, 081, 090, or 091.

Major Mobile Operators in Nigeria

  • MTN: Numbers starting with 0803, 0806, 0703, 0706, 0813, 0816, 0810, 0814, 0903
  • Airtel: Numbers starting with 0802, 0808, 0708, 0812, 0701, 0902
  • Globacom: Numbers starting with 0805, 0807, 0705, 0815, 0811, 0905
  • 9mobile: Numbers starting with 0809, 0817, 0818, 0908, 0909
