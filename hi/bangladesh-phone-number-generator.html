Bangladesh Phone Number Format

Bangladeshi mobile numbers follow the format: +880 (country code) followed by a 10-digit number starting with 1.

Major Mobile Operators in Bangladesh

  • Grameenphone: Numbers starting with 017, 018, etc.
  • Airtel: Numbers starting with 016, etc.
  • Robi: Numbers starting with 019, etc.
  • Banglalink: Numbers starting with 014, etc.
