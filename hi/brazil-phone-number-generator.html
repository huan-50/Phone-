Brazil Phone Number Format

Brazilian mobile numbers follow the format: +55 (area code) 9xxxx-xxxx, where '9' is the mobile prefix.

Major Mobile Operators in Brazil

  • Vivo: Various number ranges
  • TIM: Various number ranges
  • Claro: Various number ranges

Area Codes

Brazil uses 2-digit area codes (DDD) from 11 to 99, representing different states and regions.
